# Media Production and Planning Tool

**Type:** Professional Project · **Client:** Showrunnr · **Thumb:** `1irKxx9qHiXU2OSpq6Neja5Yvc.png`

## Hero
**Media Production and Planning Tool.** A media production management and planning tool that helps creators and providers plan, collaborate, manage, transact, and synchronize on a single platform.

## Meta
- Client: Showrunnr
- Role: UX & UI Designer
- Industry: Film & Production
- Timeline: Aug 2024 – Jul 2025

## Problem Statement
Media teams rely on scattered tools and manual processes → miscommunication, delays, budget issues. They need a single platform unifying planning, collaboration, management, and transactions.
- **Project Planning** — clear, organized planning keeping every task and timeline in sync.
- **Team & Assets** — shared space to communicate and manage all production files.
- **Smart Transactions** — instant updates and smooth transactions to keep the workflow moving.

## User Personas (two: Production & Supplier)
**Production — Cameron Williamson**, Age 27, Line Producer.
- Bio: juggles budgets, crews, schedules; struggles with scattered details and last-minute changes.
- Core needs: stay on top of multiple productions; track budgets, crew bookings, schedules in one place; get hired for relevant projects; track upcoming shoots, availability, requirements.
- Pain points: details scattered across Sheets/emails/texts; last-minute changes derail planning; no central view of crew availability/budget; uncertainty juggling parallel projects.

**Supplier — Wade Warren**, Age 40, Owner of a Set Lighting & Props Rental Company.
- Bio: runs a rental business, wants smoother bookings but slowed by poor coordination and late updates.
- Core needs: marketplace listing tools for visibility; inquiry management & booking calendar in one dashboard; collaborative space with permission-based access; timeline overviews linked to productions.
- Pain points: manual coordination via calls/emails/spreadsheets; uncertain payment timelines hurt cash flow; miscommunication causes last-minute rushes; struggles to find legitimate leads early; delayed updates on rescheduling/location changes; delays from poor planning/unclear prop requests.

## From Pain Points to Progress (two apps)
**Production Planning app** — one place for people/companies creating or managing productions to track projects and connect with resources & services.
- Keep track of production needs, schedules & logistics.
- Track the resources you need.
- Share requirements with providers and collaborate.

**Reservation Management app** — one place for people/companies providing talent, resources, or services to track bookings and connect with productions.
- End-to-end booking workflow.
- Centralized information for sharing and decision-making.
- Real-time visibility — stay in sync, track leads and bookings.

## Features — Coming Soon
**A Glimpse into One Key Feature:** prototype shows how users find people for a resource to shortlist, and how AI Summary helps finalize the shortlist.

## Conclusion
Showrunnr is evolving into a centralized hub that streamlines production workflows and improves collaboration. Still ongoing, but early insights show strong potential. Next phase: refine features, user testing, and iterate toward a seamless, production-ready platform.
