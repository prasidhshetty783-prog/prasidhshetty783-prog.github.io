# Educational Transformation: Mobile LMS

**Type:** Professional Project · **Client:** upGrad · **Thumb:** `qUuaT7anQWJj0mjtXxD2zrGDXk.png`

## Hero
**Educational Transformation: Mobile LMS.** A mobile learning management system that helps learners access courses, track progress, engage with content, and manage their learning seamlessly on the go.

## Meta
- Client: upGrad
- Role: Lead UX & UI Designer
- Industry: Education
- Timeline: Jun 2023 – Feb 2024

## Problem Statement
The company only had a desktop-supported LMS web app and wanted a mobile-first app (later to extend to desktop) addressing:
- **2 for 1** — users juggle between an admin app and the LMS app → increased cognitive load with multiple logins.
- **Old School** — unattractive UI; inconsistent icons; poor visual hierarchy.
- **Back In The Grid** — app only had pages to view courseware and assignments; lacked micro-interactions.

Quote: *"I want the app to not only look good but have features that will help me grow and study effectively."* — Subham Singh

## User Persona
**Johnny Depp**, Age 27, Part-Time Student.
- Bio: juggles budgets, crews, and schedules but struggles with scattered details and last-minute changes.
- Core needs: easy tracking of course & assignment progress; motivational nudges; reliable reminders for live sessions/due dates; bite-sized learning.
- Pain points: overwhelmed by long lectures/dense content; confusion on where to start/prioritize; disconnected experience across apps; hard to stay consistent with work/personal commitments; losing track of deadlines/submissions; demotivated when progress isn't visible.

## Wireframes
Created wireframes to outline the essential functionality of the app.

## Bringing the Vision to Life
Visual snapshots of how thoughtful design decisions transformed the app.
- **Navigation Redefined** — fresh, intuitive layout with quick links to key features.
- **Revamped Calendar: Beyond Dates** — join live sessions or rewatch past ones in a tap, in a fresher design.
- **Effortless Learning** — "Continue Learning" lets students pick up where they left off.
- **Learning Without Limits** — downloadable resources/modules for offline learning.

## Features That Make the Difference
- **Shorts: Learn Fast** — bite-sized video clips for quick insights.
- **Distraction-Free Learning** — hides navigation while studying; bookmarks, notes, page-turn effect, side index bar.
- **Learning Dashboard** — home screen shows progress, attendance, assignments (missing in the old LMS).
- **Refer & Earn** — motivates students to invite peers, boosting enrollments while rewarding learners.

## Conclusion
This project reshaped how students interact with their learning platform — a seamless, efficient experience anytime, anywhere. By unifying fee payments, queries, exam updates, and course materials into a single login, every choice focused on real student needs: simple, intuitive, useful. The foundation is ready for future upgrades like personalized recommendations and deeper learning insights.
