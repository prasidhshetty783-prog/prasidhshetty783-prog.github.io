## Problem Statement
Port operators rely on slow, fragmented, manual processes to track voyages, manage loading/unloading, and identify shipment issues → delays, errors, bottlenecks. They need a secure, device-compatible scanning app that streamlines workflows, enables quick exception handling, and supports operators in real time via an integrated AI chatbot.
- **Device Compatibility** — must run on the devices used in ports for secure, seamless use in a unique operational environment.
- **Efficiency & Exceptions** — streamline voyages, speed operations, quickly flag shipment issues with a simple exception system.
- **AI Assistant & Problem Resolution** — integrated AI chatbot gives instant guidance to resolve issues and reduce downtime.

## User Persona
**Michael Basi**, Age 40, Cargo Handler.
- Bio: seasoned cargo handler with 15 years' experience; efficient, reliable; relies on fast, clear tools to keep shipments moving.
- Core needs: quickly scan and identify shipments; track voyages and incoming loads; flag/report damaged or mismatched packages easily; reduce errors and repeated scans.
- Pain points: slow/outdated scanning systems that lag at peak hours; hard to identify problem shipments until too late; unclear system messages forcing him to ask for help; lack of real-time support for unexpected problems.

## Bringing the Vision to Life
Visual snapshots of how thoughtful design decisions transformed the app.
- **Smart Port Hub** — a single hub to track voyages, speed up loading/unloading, and spot problem shipments.
- **Instant Barcode Create** — if a label/barcode is missing, users instantly generate a new one to speed clearance.
- **Shipment Issue Hub** — report unscannable or missing labels and revisit flagged shipments to resolve issues.
- **Port Assistant** — an AI chatbot giving quick solutions to any issue faced at the port.

## Some Other Essentials
Other features that make tasks seamless: **Dark Mode**, **Port Activities**, **Measure Shipment**.

## Conclusion
The 2W Tally Scanner App reimagines port operations, enabling teams to manage packages from gate entry to ship loading. By streamlining workflows, simplifying tasks, and providing real-time visibility, the app empowers users to work efficiently, reduce errors, and drive measurable operational impact across the port.
