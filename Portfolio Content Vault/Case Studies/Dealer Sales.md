## Hero
**Empowering Dealers with Seamless Sales Management.** A sales management tool that tracks dealers' sales progress across the platform and showcases rewards, discounts & offers for dealers to enroll into.

## Meta
- Client: Asian Paints
- Role: UX & UI Designer
- Industry: E-commerce
- Timeline: Aug 2024 – Oct 2024

## Problem Statement
Designing My Awaaz came with unique challenges that shaped every decision:
- **Data Clarity** — presenting complex sales metrics and rewards without overwhelming users required careful information design.
- **Transparency** — many users struggled to understand how offers/rewards worked, so clarity and trust were top priorities.
- **Motivation & Engagement** — turning raw sales data into something that inspired dealers to perform better was a key hurdle.
- **Varying Digital Literacy** — dealers had different comfort levels with technology, demanding a simple, intuitive interface.

## Enhancements That Truly Matter
Design decisions that enhance productivity, navigation, and ease of use.
- **Simplifying Navigation** — reimagined the navigation structure and organized information for effortless access.
- **Insights Made Simple** — clean, intuitive, structured dashboard surfacing key metrics and tasks instantly.
- **Invoices Made Effortless** — neatly structured table to browse, sort, and manage invoices with speed and clarity.
- **Adapting to User Needs** — flexible design that stays clear and intuitive on both desktop and mobile.

## Conclusion
Through My Awaaz, I transformed a traditional sales tool into a modern, dealer-friendly platform. By streamlining workflows, improving navigation, and elevating the visual design, dealers can more easily track sales, access offers, and manage rewards — a more efficient, engaging system aligning user needs with business goals.
