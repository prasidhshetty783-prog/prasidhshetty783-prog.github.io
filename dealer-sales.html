Case Study — Professional Project

Empowering Dealers with Seamless Sales Management

A sales management tool that tracks dealers' sales progress across the platform and showcases rewards, discounts, and offers for dealers to enroll into.

Client
Asian Paints
Role
UX & UI Designer
Industry
E-commerce
Timeline
Aug — Oct 2024

01 Problem Statement

Challenges that shaped every decision

Designing My Awaaz came with unique challenges that shaped every design decision:

i.

Data Clarity

Presenting complex sales metrics and rewards without overwhelming users required careful information design.

ii.

Transparency

Many users struggled to understand how offers and rewards worked, so clarity and trust became top priorities.

iii.

Motivation & Engagement

Turning raw sales data into something that actually inspired dealers to perform better was a key hurdle.

iv.

Varying Digital Literacy

Dealers had different comfort levels with technology, demanding a simple and intuitive interface.

02 Information Architecture

Structuring the portal from the ground up

Created a high-level information architecture of the portal.

03 Wireframes

Outlining the essential functionality

Created the wireframes to outline the essential functionality of the app.

04 Design System

Consistency across the whole platform

Set up the design system to maintain consistency across the entire platform.

05 Enhancements That Truly Matter

Design decisions that enhance productivity

Simplifying Navigation

Reimagined the navigation structure and organized information to ensure effortless access and usability.

Insights Made Simple

A clean, intuitive, and structured dashboard that surfaces key metrics and tasks instantly.

Invoices Made Effortless

A neatly structured table that helps users browse, sort, and manage invoices with speed and clarity.

Adapting to User Needs

A flexible design that stays clear and intuitive on both desktop and mobile.

06 Conclusion

A traditional tool, made modern

Through the My Awaaz project, I transformed a traditional sales tool into a modern, dealer-friendly platform. By streamlining workflows, improving navigation, and elevating the visual design, the experience now makes it easier for dealers to track sales, access offers, and manage rewards. The result is a more efficient, engaging system that aligns user needs with business goals and delivers real value for stakeholders.
