Case Study — Professional Project

Educational Transformation: Mobile LMS

A mobile learning management system that helps learners access courses, track progress, engage with content, and manage their learning seamlessly on the go.

Client
upGrad
Role
Lead UX & UI Designer
Industry
Education
Timeline
Jun 2023 — Feb 2024

01 Problem Statement

Desktop-only learning, mobile-first lives

The company only had a desktop-supported Learning Management System (LMS) web application, and they wanted to design a mobile-first application for their users that could address the following issues — and later be implemented for the desktop web application:

i.

2 for 1

Students juggled between an administrative application and the LMS application — increased cognitive load with multiple logins.

ii.

Old School

The UI of the application was not attractive, with inconsistent use of icons and poor visual hierarchy.

iii.

Back in the Grid

The application only had pages to view courseware and assignments, and lacked micro-interactions entirely.

"I don't want an app that only looks good — it should have features that will help me grow and study effectively."
— Subham Singh, Student

02 User Persona

Learning around life, not the other way around

Subham Singh

Age 27 · Part-Time Student

Subham balances work and study, relying on his phone to keep his learning on track between personal and professional commitments.

Core Needs
  • Easy tracking of course and assignment progress.
  • Motivational nudges to stay consistent.
  • Reliable reminders for live sessions and due dates.
  • Bite-sized learning.
Pain Points
  • Overwhelmed by long video lectures and dense content.
  • Confusion about where to start and how to prioritize modules.
  • Disconnected experience across the app.
  • Losing track of deadlines, submissions, or what's incomplete.
  • Feeling demotivated when progress isn't visible.

Outlining the essential functionality

04 Bringing the Vision to Life

Thoughtful decisions that transformed the app

Navigation Redefined

A fresh, intuitive layout that lets students access key features instantly through quick links.

Revamped Calendar: Beyond Dates

The calendar now does more than list classes — students can join live sessions or rewatch past ones in a tap, within a fresher, more engaging design.

Effortless Learning

The new "Continue Learning" feature lets students pick up right where they left off, keeping their learning flow uninterrupted.

Learning Without Limits

With downloadable resources and modules, students can learn anywhere without needing constant connectivity.

05 Features That Make the Difference

Strengthening the overall learning experience

Shorts: Learn Fast

Bite-sized video clips offering quick insights to help students stay informed and learn efficiently.

Distraction-Free Learning

A focused mode hides navigation while studying; bookmarks, notes, a page-turn effect, and a side index bar make learning immersive and easy to navigate.

Learning Dashboard

The home screen now shows progress, attendance, and assignment features that were missing in the old LMS.

Refer & Earn

Motivates students to invite peers — boosting enrollments while rewarding learners. A simple win-win.

06 Conclusion

One login, one seamless experience

This project reshaped how students interact with their learning platform, creating a seamless and efficient experience they can rely on anytime, anywhere. By unifying fee payments, queries, exam updates, and course materials into a single login, every design choice focused on real student needs — making the platform simple, intuitive, and truly useful.

With a strong foundation now in place, the app is ready for future upgrades like personalized recommendations and deeper learning insights. It's a project I'm proud to have crafted — streamlining education while delivering meaningful value for both students and the business.
