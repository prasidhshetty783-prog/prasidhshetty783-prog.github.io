Port Operations & Logistics Tool

A logistics management tool transforming port operations by streamlining workflows, eliminating bottlenecks, and improving the efficiency of the workers.

01 Problem Statement

From fragmented manual processes to real-time precision

Port operators currently rely on slow, fragmented, and manual processes to track voyages, manage loading and unloading, and identify shipment issues. This leads to delays, errors, and operational bottlenecks. There is a need for a secure, device-compatible scanning application that streamlines these workflows, enables quick exception handling, and supports operators in real time through an integrated AI chatbot — ultimately improving speed, accuracy, and overall port efficiency.

i.

Device Compatibility

The app must run on the devices used in ports for secure, seamless use in the unique operational environment.

ii.

Efficiency & Exceptions

Streamline voyages, speed operations, and quickly flag shipment issues with a simple exception system.

iii.

AI Assistant & Resolution

An integrated AI chatbot provides instant guidance to help operators resolve issues quickly and reduce downtime.

02 User Persona

Designing for the deck, not the desk

Michael Basi

Age 40 · Cargo Handler

Michael is a seasoned cargo handler with 15 years of experience. He's efficient, reliable, and relies on fast, clear tools to keep shipments moving without delays.

Core Needs
  • Quickly scan and identify shipments without delays.
  • Track voyages and incoming loads efficiently.
  • Flag and report damaged or mismatched packages easily.
  • Reduce errors and avoid repeated checks or scans.
Pain Points
  • Slow or outdated scanning systems that lag during peak hours.
  • Difficulty identifying shipments with issues until it's too late.
  • Unclear system messages that force him to ask for help.
  • Lack of real-time support when encountering unexpected problems.

05 Bringing the Vision to Life

Thoughtful decisions that transformed the app

Smart Port Hub

A single hub to track voyages, speed up loading & unloading, and quickly spot shipments with issues.

Instant Barcode Create

If a shipment's label or barcode is missing, users can instantly generate a new one to speed up clearance.

Shipment Issue Hub

Users can quickly report unscannable or missing labels and revisit flagged shipments to resolve issues.

Port Assistant

An AI chatbot that gives users quick solutions to any issues they face while working at the port.

07 Conclusion

From gate to ship — measurable impact

The 2W Tally Scanner App reimagines port operations, enabling teams to manage packages from entry at the gate to loading onto ships. By streamlining workflows, simplifying tasks, and providing real-time visibility, the app empowers users to work efficiently, reduce errors, and drive measurable operational impact across the port.
